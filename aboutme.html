Rules

Pong Game

  • You Are Versing An AI
  • Move Your Mouse To Move Around Your Player
  • Hit The Ball
  • To Get A Point You Have To Make The AI Miss The Ball
  • Your Score Will Be Tracked

T-Rex Game

  • Jump Over Obstacles
  • As The Game Goes On You Will Have To Start Objects In the Sky
  • Press Space To Jump & Restart Your Game After You DIE
  • Your Score Will Be Tracked

Drawing Game

  • Click The Color And Press Your Mouse And Draw
  • The Clear Button Lets You Erase Everything
  • The White Color Button Is The Eraser
  • The Small, Medium & Large Button Wil Set The Size Of Your Drawing

Paddle Game

  • Try To Make The Blue Square Hit The Paddle
  • If The Ball Hits Any Red Block They Will Disapear
  • In Order To Win You Have To Try To Make All The Red Blocks Disapear
